    What types of electrical panels do you offer?
    We offer a range of electrical panels, including main service panels, subpanels, circuit breaker panels, and custom panels for various applications.
    Do you offer warranties on your electrical panels?
    We typically provide warranties on our electrical panels, and the duration may vary depending on the specific product. Please inquire about warranty details when making a purchase.
    Do you offer maintenance and repair services for electrical panels?
    Yes, we provide maintenance and repair services to ensure your electrical panels operate efficiently and safely. Regular inspections and servicing are recommended.
    Why would I need to upgrade my electrical panel?
    Upgrading your panel is necessary for accommodating increased power demand, ensuring safety, and meeting modern electrical code requirements.
    What signs indicate that my electrical panel needs an upgrade?
    Signs include frequent tripped breakers, flickering lights, and the panel's inability to handle the load of modern appliances.
    How do I reset a tripped breaker?
    Locate the breaker in the "off" position, then switch it to the "on" position. If it continues to trip, consult with a professional.
    Can I install new circuits in my existing electrical panel?
    Depending on the panel's capacity, additional circuits can often be added, but it's essential to consult with an electrician to ensure safety and compliance.
    Are there safety considerations when dealing with electrical panels?
    Absolutely. Always turn off the main breaker before performing any work, and if you're unsure or uncomfortable, seek professional help.
